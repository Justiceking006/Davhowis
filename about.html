Our Story

DavHowis Enterprise was founded with the same mission: to provide everyday people with extraordinary opportunities. What began as a small venture has grown into a thriving community of entrepreneurs across Nigeria and beyond. Our founder, David Horwitz, recognized the power of network marketing to transform lives by enabling individuals to build their own businesses with minimal set-up costs and maximum support. Today, thousands of members have experienced financial growth and personal development through our platform. We continue to expand our product offerings and business opportunities while maintaining our core values of integrity, quality, and community.
