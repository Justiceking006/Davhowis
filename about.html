Our Story

DavHowis Enterprise was founded with the same mission: to provide everyday people with extraordinary opportunities. What began as a small venture has grown into a thriving community of entrepreneurs across Nigeria and beyond. Our founder, David Horwitz, recognized the power of network marketing to transform lives by enabling individuals to build their own businesses with minimal set-up costs and maximum support. Today, thousands of members have experienced financial growth and personal development through our platform. We continue to expand our product offerings and business opportunities while maintaining our core values of integrity, quality, and community.

Our Mission & Values

At DavHowis Enterprise, we are guided by a set of core principles that shape everything we do.

Our Mission

To create a synergistic economic opportunity that empowers individuals to achieve financial independence through quality business. We strive to provide tools, training, and support services for our members to build entrepreneurial businesses while positively impacting their communities.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and uphold the highest ethical standards.
  • Quality: We deliver only the best products and services to our members.
  • Community: We foster a supportive and empowering community environment.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and strive for continuous improvement.
  • Empowerment: We equip individuals with tools and confidence to succeed.
